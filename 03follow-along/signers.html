Who Were the Signers

Famous Signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Independence include:

John Hancock
President of the Continental Congress, known for his bold, oversized signature.
Thomas Jefferson
Primary author of the Declaration, later 3rd U.S. President.
Benjamin Franklin
Renowned inventor, diplomat, and elder statesman.
John Adams
A strong advocate for independence, later 2nd U.S. President.
Samuel Adams
Leader of the Sons of Liberty and a key revolutionary organizer.
Richard Henry Lee
Proposed the resolution declaring independence.
Roger Sherman
Only person to sign all four of the great state papers of the U.S. (Declaration, Articles of Association, Articles of Confederation, Constitution).
Charles Carroll of Carrollton
The only Catholic signer, also the last surviving signer (died in 1832).

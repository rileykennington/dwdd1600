About Opal Haze

We started Opal Haze with a passion for style and sustainability. Every thrift find and handmade jewelry piece has a story—and now, it can be part of yours.

How We Curate

  1. We search thrift stores and estate sales for hidden gems.
  2. We restore and clean each piece with care.
  3. We match items with customers who will treasure them.

Key Terms

Upcycling
Transforming old or discarded items into something of higher quality or value.
Vintage
Clothing or items typically 20 years or older, with timeless style.
Handcrafted
Jewelry or items made by hand, not mass-produced.
